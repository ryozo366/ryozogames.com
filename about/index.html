About RyozoGames

Free browser games, built with passion.

Our Mission

RyozoGames stands for a simple idea: high-quality games should be accessible to everyone – no download, no installation, no payment. Just open your browser and start playing. We develop games that run on any device, whether desktop PC, tablet or smartphone.

In a world full of app store hurdles, in-app purchases and massive downloads, we believe there's a place for quick, fun browser games that you can play anytime, anywhere. That's exactly what we're building.

Our Games

RyozoDash is our first game – a rhythm-based platformer inspired by Geometry Dash. With three difficulty levels, procedurally generated levels and a unique neon aesthetic, it offers hours of fun. RyozoClicker is our idle clicker game – buy buildings, unlock upgrades and use the prestige system to generate coins faster and faster. And this is just the beginning!
