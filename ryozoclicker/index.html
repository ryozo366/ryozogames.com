About RyozoClicker

RyozoClicker is a free idle clicker game you can play directly in your browser. Inspired by classics like Cookie Clicker, RyozoClicker combines addictive clicking gameplay with a unique neon aesthetic. No download, no sign-up – just click and go!

Your goal: Collect as many coins as possible! Click the big neon coin in the center to earn coins. Use your coins to buy buildings that automatically generate coins for you – even when you're not clicking. The more buildings and upgrades you have, the faster your fortune grows.

Game Mechanics

MechanicDescription
ClickingClick the coin to earn coins. Upgrades increase your earnings per click.
Buildings12 different buildings from Cursor to Multiverse Core. Each building automatically generates coins per second (CPS).
Upgrades25 one-time improvements that boost your click power, building efficiency, or give special abilities like Golden Touch.
PrestigeAt 1 million coins you can do a prestige reset: reset everything, but gain a permanent multiplier.
Achievements22 achievements to unlock – from your first click to earning quadrillions.

Tips & Tricks

Click actively at the start: In the first few minutes, clicking is your main source of income. Save up for the first Cursor to start earning passive income.

Invest in buildings: Buildings generate coins even when you're not actively clicking. The more you have, the faster your fortune grows.

Don't forget upgrades: Building upgrades double a building type's CPS. This is especially worthwhile for buildings you own many of.

Use prestige: When your progress stagnates, do a prestige reset. The permanent multiplier will massively accelerate your next run.

Your progress is saved: RyozoClicker auto-saves every 30 seconds. Your progress is preserved even if you close the browser. You even earn offline (up to 24 hours)!
