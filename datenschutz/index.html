2. Overview of Data Processing

3. Hosting

This website is hosted via GitHub Pages (GitHub Inc., 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). When visiting the website, technical data is automatically collected by the hosting provider:

  • IP address of the accessing device
  • Date and time of the request

Processing is carried out pursuant to Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR based on our legitimate interest in a secure and efficient provision of the website. GitHub may process this data in the USA. The GitHub Privacy Policy applies.

4. Cookies and LocalStorage

4.1 LocalStorage

Our website uses your browser's LocalStorage for the following purposes:

  • Cookie consent (ryozogames_cookies): Stores your decision regarding cookie usage
  • Game settings (ryozodash_difficulty): Stores the selected difficulty level
  • High scores (ryozodash_best_easy, ryozodash_best_normal, ryozodash_best_hard): Stores your best scores per difficulty level
  • RyozoClicker save (ryozoclicker_save): Stores your game progress (coins, buildings, upgrades, achievements)

This data is stored exclusively locally in your browser and is not transmitted to our or third-party servers. The legal basis is Art. 6 (1) lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in the functionality of the website).

4.2 Third-Party Cookies (Google AdSense)

Advertisements on our website are served through Google AdSense (Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland). Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to display ads based on your interests and previous visits.

The following data may be processed:

  • Cookie identifiers
  • IP address (possibly anonymized)
  • Device information (screen size, browser, operating system)
  • Pages visited and interactions with ads

The legal basis for processing is your consent pursuant to Art. 6 (1) lit. a GDPR, which you can give via our cookie banner. You can revoke your consent at any time by deleting your browser cookies. Further information can be found in Google's Privacy Policy.

5. SSL/TLS Encryption

This website uses SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS) for security purposes and to protect the transmission of data. An encrypted connection is indicated by the browser's address bar changing from "http://" to "https://" and by the lock icon in your browser bar.

6. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR): You can request information about your personal data stored by us.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR): You can request the correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR): You can request the deletion of your data, unless statutory retention obligations prevent this.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR): You have the right to object at any time to the processing of your personal data.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7 (3) GDPR): You can withdraw your consent at any time with effect for the future.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.
