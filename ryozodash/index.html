About RyozoDash

RyozoDash is a free, Geometry Dash inspired platformer you can play directly in your browser. The game combines rhythm-based gameplay with a glowing neon aesthetic and procedurally generated levels. No download needed – just play!

Your goal is to safely guide the small cube through a field full of obstacles. Spikes, blocks and gaps stand in your way. With just one action – jump – you need to time the perfect moment to overcome each obstacle.

Tips & Tricks

Timing is everything: Don't jump too early or too late. Watch the obstacles and find the right rhythm.

Practice makes perfect: Every level is procedurally generated, but the obstacle patterns repeat. The more you play, the better you'll recognize the patterns.

Start on Easy: If you're new, start with Easy mode. You'll have more time to react and the obstacles are spaced further apart.

Your progress is saved: Your best score is saved locally per difficulty level, so you can always try to beat your record.
